About Our School

Ayan Montessori & School System provides a child-focused learning environment based on modern Montessori teaching methods. We aim to develop confident, disciplined, and responsible students through quality education.

Programs Offered

  • Montessori (Play Group, Nursery )
  • Primary Classes (Grade 1–5)
  • Middle Classes (Grade 6–8)
  • High School (Grade 9–10)

Admission Requirements

To apply for admission at Ayan Montessori & School System, the following documents are required:

  • Student’s Birth Certificate
  • 2 Passport-size Photographs
  • Father’s / Guardian’s CNIC Copy
  • Previous School Leaving Certificate (if applicable)
  • Last Class Marksheet (if applicable)
  • Completed Admission Form

Note: All documents must be submitted at the school office during admission hours.
